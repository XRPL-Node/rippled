619/*
620 * @brief Invariants: Vault object and MPTokenIssuance for vault shares
621 *
622 * - vault deleted and vault created is empty
623 * - vault created must be linked to pseudo-account for shares and assets
624 * - vault must have MPTokenIssuance for shares
625 * - vault without shares outstanding must have no shares
626 * - loss unrealized does not exceed the difference between assets total and
627 * assets available
628 * - assets available do not exceed assets total
629 * - vault deposit increases assets and share issuance, and adds to:
630 * total assets, assets available, shares outstanding
631 * - vault withdrawal and clawback reduce assets and share issuance, and
632 * subtracts from: total assets, assets available, shares outstanding
633 * - vault set must not alter the vault assets or shares balance
634 * - no vault transaction can change loss unrealized (it's updated by loan
635 * transactions)
636 *
637 */
