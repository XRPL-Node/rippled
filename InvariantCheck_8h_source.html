629/*
630 * @brief Invariants: Vault object and MPTokenIssuance for vault shares
631 *
632 * - vault deleted and vault created is empty
633 * - vault created must be linked to pseudo-account for shares and assets
634 * - vault must have MPTokenIssuance for shares
635 * - vault without shares outstanding must have no shares
636 * - loss unrealized does not exceed the difference between assets total and
637 * assets available
638 * - assets available do not exceed assets total
639 * - vault deposit increases assets and share issuance, and adds to:
640 * total assets, assets available, shares outstanding
641 * - vault withdrawal and clawback reduce assets and share issuance, and
642 * subtracts from: total assets, assets available, shares outstanding
643 * - vault set must not alter the vault assets or shares balance
644 * - no vault transaction can change loss unrealized (it's updated by loan
645 * transactions)
646 *
647 */
